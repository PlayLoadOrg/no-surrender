Mission Briefing
Inbound targets appear on your screen as they are detected.
Each carries a math problem. The answer is the key that fires.
Press 1–9 to fire. Hit the right answer and the round connects.
Multi-hit targets will change their problem after each strike.
You have a finite number of rounds.
When they run out, you can only watch what gets through.
Between nights, choose one upgrade.
Ammo Reload adds 10 rounds. Use it wisely.
There is no victory condition.
Every night you survive is a night someone else gets home.
